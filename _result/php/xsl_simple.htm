Farnese, Alessandro

Moved to new address,
  00120 Via del Pellegrino - Citta del Vaticano
and was re-baptized to  Paolo III  (english Paul III, spansh Paulo III)
... as say in this October 13, 1534 official announcement.

I announce to you a great joy;
We have a Pope:

The most eminent and most reverend Lord,
the Lord Alessandro
Cardinal of the Holy Roman Church Farnese
who takes to himself the name of Paul III
